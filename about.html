TECH WORLD
GLOBAL SUMMIT
2022

The Global Summit is a rally point for technologists and humanists who discuss and exchange ideas and perspectives. It is a knowledge sharing platform to make the world a better place for coming generations. Sustainability and Longevity of the human race is the sine qua non of this body and it aims to achieve the same in a befitting manner using novel methods for solving the age old problems of hunger, poverty, disease and environmental degradation.
